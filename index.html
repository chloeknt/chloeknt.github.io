Why e-invoicing?

More than 1.2 million invoices are exchanged annually in Australia. And yet, 90% are still either partially or fully manual. Not only does manual invoicing require inordinate amounts of time and budget expenses validating the invoice, the process also faces many other common problems such as misplacement, a lack of transparency or fraud vulnerability. It is predicted that switching from manual to e-invoicing will save businesses $20 in costs for each invoice. Currently, all Commonwealth agencies are on track to have the capability to receive e-invoices by 1 July 2022, with over 80 per cent of Commonwealth invoices able to be received electronically.
